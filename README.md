## 🚀 Featured Projects

### [TalentForge](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource) — AI-Powered Hiring Platform
> 🏆 **Top 3 · Smart India Hackathon 2025** · 300+ competing teams

**The problem:** Manual resume screening is slow, biased, and breaks at scale.

**The solution:** A full-stack hiring platform where NLP does the screening — TF-IDF + BERT cosine similarity scores resumes against job descriptions; Judge0 runs live coding assessments; ML ranks candidates before a human ever looks.

**Result:** ~60% reduction in manual HR effort across simulated pipelines.

`MERN` `FastAPI` `BERT Embeddings` `TF-IDF` `Cosine Similarity` `Judge0 API` `Redis` `JWT`

[![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Source-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource)

---

### [SmartCare](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource) — AI Telemedicine Platform

**The problem:** Patients carry physical reports everywhere and specialist routing is manual.

**The solution:** OCR-based report ingestion → Random Forest risk scoring → automated specialist matching → in-app WebRTC video consultation. Patients, doctors, and admins each get their own dashboard.

**Result:** 87% classifier accuracy on disease prediction; deployed live on Render.

`React` `Node.js` `MongoDB` `FastAPI` `OCR` `scikit-learn` `WebRTC` `Socket.IO` `Cloudinary`

[![Live](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-Demo-6366F1?style=flat-square&logo=vercel)](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource) [![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Source-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource)

---

### [DevRadar](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource) — AI Career Intelligence Platform
> 🏆 **Top 20 · WikiThon 2026** · AI Valley

**The problem:** Developers in India have no structured tool for skill-gap analysis, startup matching, or learning roadmap generation.

**The solution:** LLM + RAG pipeline that ingests job descriptions, URLs, and screenshots → builds a live knowledge graph → generates salary-impact skill-gap reports and week-by-week roadmaps. Groq (LLaMA 3.3 70B) primary; Claude fallback for zero-downtime reliability.

`React` `Node.js` `Groq LLaMA` `Claude AI` `HydraDB` `RAG` `vis-network`

[![Live](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-Demo-6366F1?style=flat-square&logo=vercel)](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource) [![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Source-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource)

### [CORTEX](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource) — Autonomous Infrastructure Recovery Platform

**The problem:** Production failures require manual diagnosis and intervention under pressure, introducing costly downtime.

**The solution:** Six-agent autonomous pipeline — failure detection → memory-augmented root-cause diagnosis → self-healing recovery. Zero humans in the loop. Event-driven architecture with persistent memory (HydraDB/pgvector).

`Python` `FastAPI` `React` `TypeScript` `xAI Grok` `HydraDB` `Multi-Agent Architecture` `pgvector`

[![Repo](https://img.shields.io/badge/GitHub-View_Source-181717?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://github.com/gaurav-opensource)
